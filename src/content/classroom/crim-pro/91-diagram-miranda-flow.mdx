Miranda doctrine turns on timing—custody, interrogation, warnings, invocation, and waiver. Plotting those milestones makes it easier to diagnose suppression issues in criminal procedure fact patterns.

Use the timeline to follow a suspect from investigatory stop through post-warning statements, highlighting how police must honor the right to counsel and right to silence.
