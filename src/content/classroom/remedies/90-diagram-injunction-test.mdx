Equity courts weigh multiple factors when deciding whether to grant injunctive relief. Thinking in terms of a matrix clarifies how likelihood of success, irreparable harm, balance of equities, and public interest interact.

Consult this chart when drafting or opposing a request for injunction to make sure each factor is supported with specific evidence and tailored relief.
